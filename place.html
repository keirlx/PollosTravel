places

Istanbul

Istanbul is a transcontinental city, straddling the Bosphorus strait in northwestern Turkey between the Sea of Marmara and the Black Sea. Turkey's most populous city and its cultural and financial center.

Cappadocia

A place where nature and history integrates. While geographical events created fairy chimneys. Cappadocia's landscape includes dramatic expanses of soft volcanic rock, shaped by erosion into towers, cones, valleys, and caves.

Bursa

Bursa is a city of brightly coloured houses and winding streets dotted with fountains. One of the most beautiful cities in Turkey, and it is located near Istanbul at the opposite end of the Sea of Marmara.

Trabzon

Trabzon city is one of the major historical and cultural centers of Turkey. It has cathedral-museum Hagia Sophia - a Christian church, transformed first into a mosque, and then into a museum with amazing frescoes.


Best places in Turkey

Antalya

an attractive city with shady palm-lined boulevards, a prize-winning marina on the Mediterranean. Antalya is dotted with many historic sites, from Hadrian's Gate in the city centre to the ruins of the Lycian Empire.

Pamukkale

Pamukkale, meaning "cotton castle" in Turkish, is a natural site in Denizli Province in southwestern Turkey. The area is famous for a carbonate mineral left by the flowing of thermal spring water.

Ankara

one of the driest places in Turkey and is surrounded by a barren steppe vegetation, with various Hittite, Phrygian, Hellenistic, Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman archaeological sites.

Kusadasi

set in a superb gulf in the Aegean region of Turkey and is known for its turquoise sparkling water of the Aegean Sea, broad sandy beaches, bright sun and large marina with a capacity for 600 boats.
